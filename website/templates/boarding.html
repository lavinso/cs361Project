Boarding at Rain City Dog

Safe monitored environment

Our cage free facility is monitored 24/7 and our staff are trained to handle any situation, providing a secure environment for your dog.

Comfortable Accommodations

Owner's can bring familiar items like beds, toys, and blankets. Dogs from the same family may board together.

Supervised Play

Dogs attend daycare during the day and have a restful evening in our quiet area afterwards.

Hours

Drop off must occur before 3 pm in order to allow your dog to adjust to our environment.
To avoid daycare charges the following day, pick up must occur before 10 am.
Open every day for boarding.

Requirements

  • Dogs must be older than 8 months for boarding.
  • The following vaccines are required to be up to date:
    • Bordatella (AKA kennel cough) must be renewed every 6 months
    • Rabies for dogs four months old and older
    • DHPP
    • Leptospirosis (recommended)
  • Additionally, dogs must have:
    • Record of spay/neuter
    • Proof of negative fecal test every 12 months
    • Record of spay/neuter by 8 months of age

Quick Tips

  • Some days fill up quickly, we recommend creating reservations at least a day in advance.
  • For day of reservations, call in the morning to check availability.
  • If you prefer in person or over the phone reservations to online reservations, or staff are happy to assist.

Late and Cancellation Policy

  • The after hours pick up fee is $16.
  • After hours pick up fee will be charged if pick up occurs after 7 pm on weekdays or after 5pm on weekends.
  • Morning Half day pick up ends at 1 pm. If pick up is after 1 pm, the reservation will be changed to a full day.
  • Reservations must be cancelled by 7 am the day of the reservation or a $20 cancellation fee will be charged
