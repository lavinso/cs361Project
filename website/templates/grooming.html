Grooming at Rain City Dog

Bath and Blow Out

Bath only services usually take 2 hours and include nail trims and dental care.

Haircut and Style

Cuts usually take 3 hours, include all the services in a bath and a bandana or bow.

De-matting Services

We offer de-matting for dogs with coats that need extra time to detangle. Frequent grooming reduces de-matting requirements.

Hours

Quick Tips

  • Some days fill up quickly, we recommend creating reservations at least a day in advance.
  • For day of reservations, call in the morning to check availability.
  • If you prefer in person or over the phone reservations to online reservations, or staff are happy to assist.
  • Existing daycare clients can schedule grooming sessions during a reserved daycare day. Make sure to include the details of the desired service in the reservation and pick up your pup at regular pick up.

Cancellation Policy

  • Reservations must be cancelled by 7 am the day of the reservation or a $20 cancellation fee will be charged
