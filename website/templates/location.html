Indoor Play Area

Our spacious indoor play area is perfect for any weather. Separate areas for big dogs and small dogs helps ensure safety. Staff ensure that the dogs are monitored 24 hours a day 7 days a week and that the maximum capacity is never exceeded for any area. We have separate indoor areas for large dogs, small dogs, seniors, and puppies.

Outdoor Play Area

Our outdoor zone allows dogs to burn off some steam in a different environment with fresh air, ensuring that they are engaged all day and come home tired from a full day of play.
