Services

We offer premier daycare, boarding, and grooming services. At Rain City Dog, we value safety, health and fun for every dog.

Daycare

Our daycare provides a supervised, secure, and loving environment where your dog can socialize and play all day. We have indoor and outdoor play areas and dogs of all breeds and sizes are welcome. Daycare is offered seven days a week.

Boarding

Boarding services are offered to daycare clients. Dogs are boarded crate free and a staff member is on-site 24/7. For safety reasons, we do not board dogs unfamiliar to us.

Grooming

Whether your dog needs a nail trip or a full hair cut, our expert groomers have you covered. Grooming can be scheduled during daycare or boarding or a la cart.

Add Ons

For pups that want a little extra, we have options for extra walks, treats, play time, or cuddle time. These can be added on to a daycare or boarding stay.
